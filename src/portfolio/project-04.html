Multiple of Embedded in Embedded (EiE) dev boards can synchronously and polyphonically playback MIDI song tracks. This is a final project with the EiE club which taught me how to program the dev boards which are equipped with SAM3U2 micro controller, LCD, LEDs, buzzers, a nRF51422 ANT/BLE 2.4GHz radio, and more.

How it works: The boards work how a real orchestra operates. One board is the Conductor (radio master) and all other boards playing are Instruments (radio slaves), the Conductor provides the music data to each of the instrument boards and provides timing to ensure all boards play synchronously. Once all boards have the music data playback starts, when the Conductor gives the cue. Learn more on my Github!
