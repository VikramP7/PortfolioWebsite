Who I Am

Second Year Eng Student at UofC

I am a second-year electrical engineering student motivated by my curiosity to learn, and by work on hands on projects. I am minoring in biomedical engineering and love to program. My passions lie in the intersection between the electrical/programming world and the medical world.

Ever since I was 11 years old, I was tinkering with electronics and programming random projects I was curious about. I'd fill my time learning about CAD design, programming, web dev, circuitry, and anything else my parents or a YouTube video could teach me.

If I'm not behind my keyboard or a soldering iron, you'll likely find me in front of the piano or in the kitchen. I love playing music and I love to bake. Playing piano or trumpet, or trying a new cookie recipe is my escape.
